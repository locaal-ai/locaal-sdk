Finds the starting indices of the overlapping region between two sequences of whisper token data.

This function compares two sequences of whisper token data and identifies the starting indices where the sequences begin to overlap. It returns a pair of integers representing the starting indices of the overlap in the first and second sequences, respectively.

seq2The second sequence of whisper token data.
Returns
